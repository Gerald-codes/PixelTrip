# PixelTrip — Product Overview

## What It Is

PixelTrip is a collaborative AI travel planning web app for groups of friends. Users join a shared trip room using a room code, choose an 8-bit character persona that represents their travel style, and then work together through an AI-assisted pipeline to choose a destination, build a fair itinerary, and negotiate changes.

## Who It's For

Small groups (2–6 people) planning a trip together who struggle to agree on destination, budget, and activities. The primary user is a trip organiser (the host) and their friends (trip members).

## Core Differentiators

1. **Agentic destination suggestions** — The AI recommends destinations based on real factors: season, weather conditions, crowd levels, estimated price, and group persona fit — not just popularity.
2. **Persona-driven fairness** — Each user's 8-bit persona (Foodie Boss, Scenic Wanderer, Master Planner, Chill Explorer, Luxury Traveller) directly influences the itinerary. The system tracks and surfaces whether each persona was fairly represented.
3. **Feedback and negotiation loop** — After the itinerary is generated, users score it, request changes, and the AI mediates conflicts and proposes compromises. The group votes on resolutions.

## What It Is Not

- Not a real booking platform (no payments, no actual reservations)
- Not a solo travel planner
- Not a native mobile app
- Not a general itinerary template generator

## MVP North Star

The demo should make it obvious that PixelTrip is fundamentally different from a standard itinerary generator. The three moments that must land:
1. The destination recommendation explains *why* a place is or isn't right for this group at this time.
2. The generated itinerary visibly reflects each person's character.
3. When someone is unhappy, the AI helps the group negotiate — not just regenerate blindly.
